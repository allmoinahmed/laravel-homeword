    What is the Feasibility Study of the SDLC model:
        * A Feasibility Study is an evaluation of the potential impact of a proposed project or system, in terms of its technical, economic, and operational viability. This is one of the most important part of a project/product etc.
    
    Why is this important?
    It helps to find or understand if a product/project if economical or technically possible / Feasibile. Also this help to find any potential risk or limitation.
